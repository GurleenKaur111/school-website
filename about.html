Our motto is "Knowledge is Power"

The aim of the school is to create an academic and physical environment of a nature, which helps the children to become ideal members of the society, develop skills and knowledge which is the envy of all and lastly, have a disciplined character embodied with the spiritual values of God.

The media of teaching in the school is English. The aim of the school is to tranform a child who is always punctual, disciplined, regular, sincere, honest, courteous, properly behaved and use english as the means of communication.
